Evolution

First Exposure to Technology

My journey began with basic computer exploration. I learned how digital tools worked through everyday use of computers, applications, and games. This stage built my curiosity about technology and how systems function.

First Attempts at Building

I started experimenting with basic web development concepts, learning HTML and CSS and exploring how websites were created. During this period, I also became curious about cybersecurity and began developing a deeper interest in understanding digital systems.

Entrepreneurship & The Need to Build

My interest in entrepreneurship began during this period. I wanted to create solutions and build products, but I realized I needed technical skills to turn ideas into reality. That realization pushed me toward learning software development and understanding how technology could become a tool for creating businesses.

Exploration & Foundation Building

After getting my first PC, I began seriously exploring software development. I experimented with different technologies, repeatedly challenged myself to learn, and built an understanding of programming concepts, systems, and digital products. Although the traditional coding journey was challenging, this period gave me something valuable: broad exposure to how technology works.

Structured Learning & AI Discovery

I joined Creative IT Institute to strengthen my technical foundation while continuing to explore emerging technologies. I began experimenting with AI tools, development workflows, and new approaches to building software. This became the transition from learning technology to using technology as a creation tool.

From Concepts to Real Products

With years of accumulated exposure across technology, business, and problem-solving, I began building complete digital ecosystems. When AI-native development workflows became possible, I was able to combine my previous learning, product understanding, and system thinking to move quickly from ideas to working products. Today, I design and build AI-powered ecosystems across industries including fitness, healthcare, travel, broadcasting, and automation.
